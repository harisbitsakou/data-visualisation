Visualisation of U.S Departments Project Management Metrics

by Charikleia Bitsakou

*This analysis is part of a coursework, and the dataset used does not contain accurate data

Description

The following bubble chart was created to presents project management information for the 15 executive departments of the United States administration, between the period of 2006 to 2012.

Audience

This visualisation is intended for the United States administration, and aims to help them identify the executive departments that on average excede their initial schedule and budget when carrying out a project.
